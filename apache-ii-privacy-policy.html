Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

All data entered into the App:

  • Is entered voluntarily by the user
  • Is processed locally on the device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not stored or backed up

2. Health Data

The App allows users to input clinical values (e.g., vital signs, laboratory results) solely to calculate the APACHE II score.

  • No health data is saved
  • No health data is shared
  • No health data is used for diagnosis or treatment

3. Purpose of Data Use

Entered data is used exclusively to:

  • Calculate the APACHE II score
  • Display the calculated result

No analytics, tracking, profiling, or advertising tools are used.

4. Medical Disclaimer

This application is intended for educational and informational purposes only.

  • It is not a medical device
  • It does not provide medical advice
  • Clinical decisions must be made by qualified healthcare professionals

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • External APIs that collect user data

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store any personal or health data, there is no risk of data breaches related to user information.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
